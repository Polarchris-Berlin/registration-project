Important Information

Explore Norway offers three distinct categories of group tours. Here you will find more details to help you choose the registration category that best suits your booked trip.

Our destination locations often provide unforgettable experiences, such as a fantastic northern lights display against the royal blue glow of the polar sky. But we also strive to do our best to contribute to an unforgettable journey with extraordinary experiences. We aim to offer you the best possible service, from selecting your trip to the completion of your journey. By completing our online registration, you will help us ensure that every detail of your upcoming trip is perfectly prepared. Together with our trusted partners, we will strive to fulfill your wishes to the best of our ability.

We thank you for your time and look forward to the shared experiences that lie ahead. Please do not hesitate to contact us if you have any further questions.

Registration Types

  • Coastal Express

    For guests who have booked a group coastal express voyage with us aboard the Hurtigruten or Havila ships along the Norwegian coast.

  • Expedition

    For guests who have chosen our polar expeditions, such as to Svalbard, Greenland, or Antarctica.

  • All other tours

    For all other group tours in our portfolio, please use this option. This registration is optimized for short trips, rail round trips, or group tours to special destinations.
